Baked Falafel with Silk-Y Smooth Vegan Tzatziki

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Servings: Servings 12 Source: Theveglife.com

Ingredients

FOR THE FALAFEL:

1 cup Canned Chickpeas drained and rinsed

1 tbsp Tahini

2 tbsp Onion diced

1/2 tsp Garlic Powder

1/2 tsp Ground Cumin

1 c Fresh Parsley

1 tbsp Lemon Juice

1/4 tsp Salt

1/8 tsp Black Pepper


FOR THE VEGAN TZATZIKI DIPPING SAUCE:

1 container Silk Plain Almond Dairy - Free Yogurt Alternative (5.3 oz)

2 tbsp Silk Unsweetened Almondmilk (if necessary to thin)

1/4 c Cucumber diced

1 tsp Dill

2 tsp Lemon Juice

Salt & Pepper to taste

Garlic Powder to taste

Directions

Whisk the ingredients for the tzatziki together and place in the refrigerator until ready to use.

In the bowl of a food processor, add all of the ingredients and pulse until roughly combined. It should hold together well, but if it's too moist, add 1 tbsp of flour.

Using a cookie scoop, place onto a line cookie sheet. Using damp hands, you can shape them if necessary. Try not to handle them too much.

Bake at 350 degrees for about 12 minutes (oven temperatures and times vary, but the bottoms will be golden and they will be cooked through).

Serve with wedges of lemons, tomatoes and our tzatziki over a bed of butter lettuce.
