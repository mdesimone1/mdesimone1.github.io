Mustard Orzo Salad

Total Time: 10 min Servings: 2 servings Source: Meals.whatthehealthfilm.com

Ingredients

1c. Whole wheat orzo

3T. water

2t. mustard

1t. paprika

½t chili powder

4c baby kale

¼c fresh parsley

1 Red pepper

½ Lime

1c. No-salt canned black beans


Salt & pepper to taste

Directions

Cook the whole wheat orzo according to the directions on the package. When ready, drain quickly to prevent overcooking the orzo pasta.

In a small bowl, combine the water (measurement specified in the ingredient list) and mustard. Add paprika and chili powder if you have some handy, but these ingredients aren't essential. Whisk ingredients together and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Produce Prep: Rinse all the produce. Mince the baby kale and fresh parsley into tiny pieces. Remove the stem and the seeds from the red pepper. Dice the red pepper. Cut the lime into wedges.

Drain and rinse the canned black beans.

In a larger bowl, mix the fresh produce with the whole wheat orzo, beans and dressing. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Notes

Base recipe serves 2
