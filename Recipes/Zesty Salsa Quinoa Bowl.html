Zesty Salsa Quinoa Bowl

Servings: 4 servings Source: my.forksmealplanner.com

Ingredients

2 cups quinoa

1 1/2 cups salsa

(divided)

10 oz fresh or frozen corn

(≈2 cups)

1 (15-oz) can pinto beans

sea salt

freshly ground black pepper

FOR SERVING

10 oz cherry tomatoes

1 avocado

chopped

4 scallions

(white & green parts) sliced (≈ 1/2 cup)

1/2 cup fresh cilantro

1 lime

Directions

In a medium saucepan over high heat, combine quinoa, 1 cup salsa, and 3 1/2 cups water.Cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until quinoa is tender, 15 to 20 minutes.

Stir in corn and pinto beans and cook until heated through, 1 to 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Portion quinoa into bowls. Top with tomatoes, avocado, remaining salsa, scallions, and cilantro. Serve alongside lime wedges.
