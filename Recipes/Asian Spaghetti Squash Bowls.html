Asian Spaghetti Squash Bowls

Total Time: 15 min Servings: 4 servings Source: yummly.co.uk

Ingredients

1 spaghetti squash (Medium)

1/4 cup soy sauce (Braggs, or coconut aminos to make paleo)

6 cloves garlic (minced)

1 tablespoon turbinado sugar (or coconut sugar)

2 teaspoons ginger (freshly grated)

1/4 teaspoon white pepper

1 tablespoon ghee (or Coconut Oil)

1 onion (Medium, diced)

3 stalks celery (sliced diagonally)

12 ounces slaw mix (Broccoli, I bought a bag from the store, but you can grate your own)

Sriracha

hot sauce

3 green onions (sliced diagonally, optional)

2 sprigs cilantro (optional)

chili garlic sauce (optional)
