Oat Flax Pancakes

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Source: nourishinggurus.com

Ingredients

Ingredients Version 1

1 ½ cups Rolled Oats

¼ cup Plain Protein Powder (pumpkin seed, hemp seed, Vital Proteins, or other)

1 ¼ cups Almond Milk, unsweetened

1 large Ripe Banana

½ tsp Cinnamon, ground

1 heaping tbsp Raw Honey

¼ tsp Sea Salt

1 tsp Vanilla Extract, alcohol free

1 ½ tsp Baking Powder

1 large Organic Egg

Coconut Oil OR Ghee (for cooking)

Berries (optional)


Ingredients Version 2

1 ½ cups Gluten Free Oats

1 heaping tbsp Flaxseeds, ground

¼ cup Protein Powder (pumpkin seed, hemp seed, Vital Proteins, or other)

1 cup Almond Milk, unsweetened

1 tsp Cinnamon, ground

1 tsp Ginger Powder

1 heaping tbsp Raw Honey

¼ tsp Sea Salt

1 tsp Vanilla Extract, alcohol free

1 ½ tsp Baking Powder

2 Organic Eggs

Coconut Oil or Ghee (for cooking)

1 ½ cups Fresh Fruit, diced (bananas, berries, peaches, etc)

Directions

Place all ingredients, except oil/butter in a blender and blend until smooth, about 20 seconds.

VERSION 2: Add fresh fruit at this stage into the batter (optional) and mix with wooden spoon.

Heat a griddle or large sauté pan over medium heat and melt a couple teaspoons of cooking fat.

When hot, pour the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

Brown on both sides (about 2-3 minutes per side).

If batter becomes too thick to pour easily, add a tablespoon or two of almond milk to thin.

Serving suggestions:

> With a drizzle of maple syrup (1 teaspoon per serving)

> Add some berries right to the batter (after blending) and cook into the pancake

> Pick up pancake like a tortilla/taco, fill with berries, fold and eat

> Spread pancake with thin layer of almond/cashew butter (and a layer of berries if you like); top with another pancake, and eat like breakfast sandwich!

> Topped with fresh berries and/or sliced banana or peaches and chopped nuts like pecans, walnuts or slivered almonds

> Use a pancake like a piece of bread to scoop up some scrambled eggs
