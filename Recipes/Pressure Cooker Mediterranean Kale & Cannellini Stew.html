Pressure Cooker Mediterranean Kale & Cannellini Stew

Source: my.forksmealplanner.com

Ingredients

3/4 yellow onion

4 1/2 cloves garlic minced (≈1 1/2 tablespoons)

1 lb + 2 oz potatoes scrubbed & cut into large dice (≈3 cups)

1 1/2 (15-oz) cans cannellini beans

1 1/2 (15-oz) cans low-sodium fire-roasted tomatoes

3 carrots

1 1/2 stalks celery cut into medium dice (≈2 1/4 oz)

1/4 (32-fl oz) carton low-sodium vegetable broth

1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil

3/4 teaspoon dried rosemary

1 1/2 dried bay leaves

3 oz kale

sea salt

freshly ground black pepper

FOR SERVING

6 oz whole-grain baguettes

Directions

In a pressure cooker, use the sauté setting to cook onions and garlic, stirring occasionally, for 2 to 3 minutes. Add water 1 to 2 tablespoons at a time as needed, to keep vegetables from sticking.

Add potatoes, undrained cannellini beans, fire-roasted tomatoes with their juices, carrots, celery, vegetable broth, basil, rosemary, and bay leaf. Lock lid in place. Set to high pressure to cook for 5 minutes.

Do a quick release to release pressure. Open lid carefully. Switch to sauté setting. Add kale and cook until wilted and tender, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove and discard bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Serve with crusty whole-grain baguette.
