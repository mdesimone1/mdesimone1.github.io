Easy Vegan Stuffed Mushrooms

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: Yield: 12 Source: theminimalistvegan.com

Ingredients

12 cremini or button mushrooms, centres removed and gills/ribs scooped out with a teaspoon*

Half of the garlic parsley sauce

3 tbsp raw cashews

⅓ cup nutritional yeast

⅓ cup vegan panko or normal breadcrumbs*

Pepper to taste

Lemon juice (optional)

Directions

Preheat oven to 200C (390F). Place the prepared mushrooms on a sheet pan or flat baking tray.

In a food processor, add in the mushroom centre and gills, garlic parsley sauce ingredients, and cashews and pulse until you get a nice crumb.

In a small skillet on medium-high heat, add the processed ingredients and saute for a few minutes.

Remove from the heat and stir in the nutritional yeast, breadcrumbs and pepper.

Stuff the mushrooms with the mix evenly (around 2 tsp worth) and bake for 20 minutes.

Remove from oven once the topping starts to slightly brown. If you like it a little more crispy on the top, you can turn the grill setting on for 1-2 minutes at the end.

Squeeze a few drops of fresh lemon juice onto each mushroom and serve.

Notes

* If you want a meatier mushroom with every bite, don't scoop out the gills.

* Make sure you check the ingredients of the breadcrumbs, sometimes they can have milk solids, honey, or tuna oil. Ideally, use homemade.
