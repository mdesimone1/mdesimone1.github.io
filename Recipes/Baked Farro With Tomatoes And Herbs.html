Baked Farro With Tomatoes And Herbs

★★★★★

Source: Veganomicon

Ingredients

2T. olive oil

2c. uncooked farro

2 cloves garlic, minced

1/2c. finely chopped shallot

1 bay leaf

2c. vegetable broth

1 28-ounce can diced tomatoes (do not drain)

2t. dried oregano

1t. dried thyme or basil

1/2t. salt (or to taste)

1/2c. chopped parsley (or basil)

2T. nutritional yeast


Topping

1/2 c. chopped walnuts

1/4 c. nutritional yeast

1/2 t. salt

1/2 t. grated lemon zest

2T. olive oil

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 and lightly greast 9x13 dish with olive oil.

Sort, pick and rinse farro

In a large saucepan over medium, sauté the garli and shallot in 1T olive oil to soften the shallot.
Stir in the farro and bay leaf and sauté another minute, then pour in vegetable broth and tomatoes.
Bring to rapid simmer, then cook for 2 minutes.

Turn off head and add oregano, thyme, and salt. Remove bay leaf.

Stir in parsley and nutritional yeast.

Pour farro mixure into baking dish and smooth top. Cover with foil and bake 40 minutes, or until almost all liquid is absorbed.

Pulse together topping ingredients except oil into coarse crumbs. Remove casserole, remove foil & cover with topping. Drizzle 2T olive oil on top & bake for 10 minutes until lightly brown. Remove from oven & let sit for 10 minutes to cool & firm up.
