Grilled Zucchini with Tomato-Mint Relish

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Servings: Servings: 4 Source: eatingwell.com

Ingredients

4 small zucchini (about 1 pound), halved lengthwise

1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil plus 1 teaspoon, divided

½ teaspoon ground pepper, divided

¼ teaspoon salt, divided

1 cup chopped seeded tomatoes

1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint

1 teaspoon red-wine vinegar

Directions

Instructions Checklist

Preheat grill to medium-high.

Brush both sides of zucchini with 1 tablespoon oil and sprinkle the cut side with 1/4 teaspoon pepper and 1/8 teaspoon salt. Grill, turning once halfway, until tender, 6 to 8 minutes.

Meanwhile, combine tomatoes, mint, vinegar, and the remaining 1 teaspoon oil, 1/4 teaspoon pepper and 1/8 teaspoon salt in a small bowl.

Serve the zucchini topped with the tomato-mint relish.
