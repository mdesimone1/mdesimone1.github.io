Zoodle Marinara

Source: my.forksmealplanner.com

Ingredients

FOR THE MARINARA:

1 (8-oz) package sliced button mushrooms (≈3 cups)

2 carrots

cut into small dice (≈1 cup)

1 yellow onion

chopped (≈1 cup)

1 green bell pepper

chopped (≈1 cup)

6 cloves garlic

minced (≈2 tablespoons)

1 1/2 cups marinara sauce

1 1/2 (8-oz) cans low-sodium tomato sauce (≈1 1/2 cups)

2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

sea salt

freshly ground black pepper

12 oz whole-grain linguine

1 (12-oz) package zucchini noodles (see tip) (≈12 oz)

FOR THE SALAD:

4 pears cut into wedges

2 oz salad greens (≈2 cups)

1 cup orange juice

2 tablespoons walnuts (optional) chopped

Directions

SAUTÉ AROMATICS

In a large skillet over medium heat, cook mushrooms, carrots, onions, bell peppers, and garlic, stirring occasionally, until onions start to turn translucent, 3 to 4 minutes. Add water 1 to 2 tablespoons at a time as needed, to keep vegetables from sticking.

MAKE SAUCE

Add marinara sauce, tomato sauce, and Italian seasoning to aromatics. Simmer, uncovered, until vegetables are very tender and sauce has thickened slightly, 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

COOK PASTA

Meanwhile, cook linguine according to package instructions. Add zucchini noodles during the last 1 minute of cooking. Drain well.

MAKE SALAD

In a bowl, combine pears, greens, and orange juice. Toss well. Scatter walnuts over, if desired.

SERVE

Top noodles with sauce. Serve alongside salad.
