The Best Vegan Taco Filling

Servings: Yield: 6 servings Source: veginspired.com

Ingredients

1/2 cup french lentils , (you can sub black lentils, but french are the best)

3 cups water

1 onion, finely diced

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 teaspoon ground coriander

1 tablespoon ground cumin

2 teaspoon chili powder

2 teaspoon smoked paprika

ground cayenne pepper (to taste - optional)

1/2 cup bulgar

1 1/2 cups water

1 teaspoon brown sugar, *sub your favorite sweetener or omit

2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar

salt to taste

Taco Shells

Your favorite taco toppings

Directions

Cook lentils in 3 cups of water, bring to a simmer and simmer for 20 minutes. You want them to be tender, yet toothsome.

Saut√© finely diced onion 5-7 minutes until translucent. Add 2 minced garlic cloves.

Add spices: cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, ground coriander, ground cayenne pepper (optional to taste), salt to taste

Add bulgar & 1 1/2 cups of water

Bring to simmer and cook, covered on low, until bulgar is tender, 12 minutes.

Add brown sugar, apple cider vinegar and salt to taste.

Add cooked lentils. ** you may adjust for consistency, we add a little water to make it saucier.

Make your tacos with your favorite toppings.
