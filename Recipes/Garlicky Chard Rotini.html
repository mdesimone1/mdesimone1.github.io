Garlicky Chard Rotini

Servings: 4 servings Source: my.forksmealplanner.com

Ingredients

1/2 cup whole-wheat panko breadcrumbs

1/4 cup walnuts

2 teaspoons lemon zest

2 yellow onions

chopped (≈2 cups)

2 carrots

chopped (≈1 cup)

6 cloves garlic

minced (≈2 tablespoons)

1/4 (32-oz) carton low-sodium vegetable broth

(≈1 cup)

10 1/2 oz Swiss chard

1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

1 (15-oz) can cannellini beans

12 oz whole-grain rotini

sea salt

freshly ground black pepper

FOR SERVING

1 lemon

Directions

In a small skillet over medium heat, cook panko and walnuts until lightly toasted, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in lemon zest. Set aside.

In a large skillet over medium heat, cook onions, carrots, and garlic, stirring occasionally, for 4 to 5 minutes. Add vegetable broth 1 to 2 tablespoons at a time as needed, to keep vegetables from sticking.

Add Swiss chard and pepper flakes to skillet and cook, adding vegetable broth as needed, until chard has wilted, 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in cannellini beans and enough vegetable broth to help reach a saucy consistency.

Meanwhile, cook rotini according to package instructions. Drain well, reserving pasta cooking liquid.

Toss rotini with chard and cannellinis, adding pasta cooking liquid if needed to help rotini get nice and saucy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Top rotini with panko and walnuts, then serve alongside lemon wedges.

Notes

Do not toss pasta with panko until ready to serve.

Swiss chard: You can substitute stemmed and chopped kale for the chard.
