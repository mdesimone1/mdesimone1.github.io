Impossible Meat Balls

Source: impossiblefoods.com

Ingredients

12 oz package impossible burger

1/4 cup diced onions

2 cloves minced garlic

2 T. breadcrumbs

1 egg

2 T. grated cheese

1 t. thyme

1 t. oregano

1 t. basil

1 t. red pepper

1 t. salt

1/2 t. black pepper

Directions

Preheat oven to 425
Mix ingredients until incorporated
Make 2 oz meatballs
Place on baking sheet lined with parchment paper
Bake 8-12 minutes until golden brown
