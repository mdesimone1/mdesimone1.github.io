The Best Dry Rub for Ribs

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 5 mins Servings: Servings 12 servings Source: simplyrecipes.com

Ingredients

3/4 cup packed dark brown sugar (or 1/2 cup if you prefer a more savory rub)

2 tablespoons kosher salt

2 tablespoons onion powder

2 tablespoons smoked paprika

1 tablespoon dry mustard

1 tablespoon granulated garlic

1 tablespoon ground black pepper

1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

1 teaspoon allspice

Directions

Mix:

Place all the spices in an airtight glass jar and shake gently to combine.

Store:

Store in an airtight container for up to a year.
