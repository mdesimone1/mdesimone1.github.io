Vegan Gravy

Ingredients

1c. Veggie broth

2T tamari

1t garlic powder

1/8t dried thyme

1/4t dried sage


1T arrowroot powder

3T water

Description

https://www.instagram.com/p/B6GjQrJn1Wt/?igshid=1vevxxu0jpcti

Directions

Add all except arrowroot to a pot.
Bring to a boil and simmer for 2 minutes.

Mix in arrowroot powder with 3T cold water and pour into gravy.

Whisk to thicken; simmer 3 additional minutes, then serve.
