Creamy Tomato Basil Spaghetti

Servings: 4 servings Source: my.forksmealplanner.com

Ingredients

FOR THE PASTA:

1 yellow onion

6 cloves garlic

3 cups unflavored plant milk

1/2 (32-fl oz) carton low-sodium vegetable broth

12 oz whole-grain spaghetti

broken up

1 lb + 4 oz cherry tomatoes

halved (≈4 cups)

4 oz kale

stemmed & thinly sliced (≈4 cups)

1/2 cup fresh basil

sea salt

freshly ground black pepper

FOR THE CHEESY TOPPING:

1/3 cup raw, unsalted cashews

2 tablespoons nutritional yeast

1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions

In a stockpot over medium heat, cook onions and garlic, stirring occasionally, until onions start to turn translucent, 2 to 3 minutes. Add water 1 to 2 tablespoons at a time as needed, to keep vegetables from sticking.

Add plant milk, vegetable broth, and spaghetti to stockpot. Increase heat to high, cover, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until spaghetti is almost tender, 7 to 10 minutes.

Meanwhile, in a food processor (or high-powered blender), process cashews, nutritional yeast, and garlic powder until very fine. Set aside.

Stir cherry tomatoes, kale, and half the basil into pasta. Cook until tomatoes are heated through and spaghetti is tender. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Sprinkle spaghetti with remaining basil and cheesy topping.
