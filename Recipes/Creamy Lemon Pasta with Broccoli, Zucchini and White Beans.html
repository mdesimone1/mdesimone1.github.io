Creamy Lemon Pasta with Broccoli, Zucchini and White Beans

Total Time: 25 min Servings: 2 servings Source: Meals.whatthehealthfilm.com

Ingredients

1 head Fresh broccoli

1 Zucchini

1 Lemon

2 cloves garlic

1/3 cup + 2 tsps Cashews

1 t. Low-sodium soy sauce/tamari

1 cup Unsweetened soy/nut/rice milk

2 tbsps nutritional yeast

1 c. Pasta (whole-grain or gluten-free)

2t. Olive oil

Â½ c. No-salt canned cannellini beans

Directions

Produce Prep: Rinse the fresh broccoli and zucchini.
Cut the fresh broccoli and zucchini into bite-size pieces, removing the woody ends.

Cut the lemon in half and squeeze out the juice in a separate container, removing the seeds as you do so. Peel the garlic.

In a food processor or blender, combine the cashews, half of the lemon juice, unsweetened soy/nut/rice milk, garlic, soy sauce and nutritional yeast. Blend until smooth.

Prepare the whole grain pasta according to the directions on the box.

In a large pan or wok, add the olive oil and bring up to medium heat. (To make this recipe oil-free, omit the olive oil and use water.) Add the fresh broccoli and zucchini and saute for 3 minutes.

Add the sauce to the pan with the vegetables. Sitr it in, and then reduce heat to low. Simmer for a few more minutes, stirring frequently, until the sauce has thickened somewhat.

Rinse and drain the canned cannellini beans.

Remove the sauce mixture from heat. Add the beans and drained pasta to the pan and stir to coat. Pour the remaining lemon juice over the pasta and stir to combine. Serve warm!
