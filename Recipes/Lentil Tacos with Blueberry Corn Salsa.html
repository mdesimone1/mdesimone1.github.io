Lentil Tacos with Blueberry Corn Salsa

Servings: 6 servings Source: my.forksmealplanner.com

Ingredients

FOR THE LENTILS:

2 cups dry brown lentils

1/3 cup fresh lime juice

FOR SERVING

18 corn tortillas

1 1/2 avocados

Directions

In a stockpot over high heat, combine lentils, onions, bell peppers, taco seasoning, and 5 1/4 cups water. Cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until tender, 25 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Meanwhile, working over a large bowl, use a knife to carefully slice kernels from corn. Add blueberries, jalape√±os (if using), scallions, cilantro, and lime juice. Mix well.

In a skillet over medium heat, heat tortillas until pliable, about 20 seconds on each side.

Use a slotted spoon to portion lentil filling into each tortilla. Top with blueberry salsa and avocado slices.
