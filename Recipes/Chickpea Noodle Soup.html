Chickpea Noodle Soup

Total Time: 25 min Servings: 2 servings Source: Meals.whatthehealthfilm.com

Ingredients

½ Onion

1 clove Garlic

2 Carrot

2 Celery stalk

1 c. Spinach

1c. No-salt canned garbanzo beans

4c. Low-sodium vegetable stock/broth

1 c. Pasta (whole-grain or gluten-free)

1T. White miso paste

2T. nutritional yeast


Optional

½ Avocado

Salt & pepper to taste

2 slices Bread (whole-grain or gluten-free)

Directions

Produce Prep: Slice the avocado lengthwise, all the way around the pit. Gently twist each side to open the avocado. Scoop out flesh and cut into small pieces. Peel and finely chop the onion and garlic. Rinse the carrot and celery stalks and chop into bite-size pieces. Roughly chop the spinach. Drain and rinse the beans.

Heat soup pot over medium and add a few tablespoons of water. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Water sauté until veggies soften (about 5 minutes).

Add stock/broth and bring to a boil. Once boiling, add the pasta.

When the pasta is almost cooked, stir in the white miso paste, nutritional yeast and beans. When pasta is tender, turn off the heat and fold in the spinach until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.

If desired, serve with avocado toast. Toast the bread and spread with the avocado.
