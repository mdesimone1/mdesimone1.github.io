Dry Beans In Pressure Cooker And Conversions

Ingredients

dry black beans or chickpeas

Directions


DRIED BEANS  yields COOKED BEANS
1/3 cup dry = 1 cup cooked
1/2 cup dry = 1 1/2 cups cooked
2/3 cup dry = 2 cups cooked
1 cup dry beans = 3 cups cooked
2 cups (1 lb) dry = 6 cups cooked

Pressure cooker
Black beans: 20 minutes, natural release
Water is 4:1, bay leaf, 1-2t. salt
25 minutes is soft

Chickpeas: 22 minutes for fully cooked;;cover with one inch of water; 25 is soft

Brown chockpeas - 30 minutes
Navy beans: 34 minutes


CANNED BEANS TO COOKED BEANS

14 -16 oz can = 1.5 cups cooked beans
19 oz can = 2¼ cups cooked beans
28 oz can = 3 – 3¼ cups cooked beans
DRY BEAN YIELDS AFTER COOKING

1 pound dry beans = 6 to 7 cups cooked beans, drained
1 pound dry beans = about 2 cups dry beans
1 cup dry beans (most kinds) = 3 cups cooked beans
⅔ cup dry beans (most kinds) = 2 cups cooked beans
½ cup dry beans (most kinds) = 1½ cups cooked beans
⅓cup dry beans = 1 cup cooked beans
