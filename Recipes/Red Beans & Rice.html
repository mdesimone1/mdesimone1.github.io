Red Beans & Rice

Servings: 3 servings Source: my.forksmealplanner.com

Ingredients

3/4 cup brown rice

1 1/2 red or green bell peppers

chopped (≈1 1/2 cups)

1 yellow onion

chopped (≈1 cup)

3 cloves garlic

1 cup low-sodium vegetable broth

3/4 (15-oz) can low-sodium diced tomatoes

2 tablespoons tomato paste

2 1/4 teaspoons dried oregano

3/4 teaspoon smoked paprika

1 1/2 (15-oz) cans red kidney beans

sea salt

freshly ground black pepper

3 tablespoons fresh parsley

Louisiana-style hot sauce

Directions

In a saucepan, bring rice and 1 1/2 cups water to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool; fluff rice with a fork.

In a saucepan over medium heat, cook bell peppers, onions, and garlic, stirring occasionally, until onions start to turn translucent, 3 to 4 minutes. Add vegetable broth 1 to 2 tablespoons at a time as needed, to keep vegetables from sticking to pan.

Add remaining vegetable broth, tomatoes with their juices, tomato paste, oregano, and smoked paprika. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.

Add kidney beans and cooked rice and continue to cook until very hot, 5 to 10 minutes.

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Serve topped with parsley and hot sauce, if desired.
