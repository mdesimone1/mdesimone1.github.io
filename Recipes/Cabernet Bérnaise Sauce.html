Cabernet Bérnaise Sauce

Ingredients

3/4 c. butter

2 T minced shallots

2 t minced fresh tarragon or 3/4 t dried tarragon

1/2 c. Cabernet Sauvignon or other dry red wine

3 egg yolks

1-2T lemon juice

Directions

1. In a 1 to 1 1/2 quart pan over low heat, melt butter and keep hot

2. In a 10 to 12 inch frying pan over high heat, boil shallots, tarragon, wine and roast drippings stirring often until liquid is reduced to about a quarter cup, 5 to 7 minutes. Keep hot.

3. Turn heat under butter too high and heat to 230° (foam begins to brown), about five minutes.

4. Put egg yolks in a blender and whirl at high-speed; and shallot mixture, then gradually pour in butter, adding it all in about 10 seconds. Stir in lemon juice to taste. Serve hot or at room temperature.
